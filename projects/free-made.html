Free Made Bringing Awareness to Everyday Purchases (2016 - 2017)

Free Made

Free Made is an app that scans bar codes and tells the user if slave labor went into the production of the item.

While this project still has not seen the light of day, it has served as a learning opportunity.

It is my hope to one day return to this project not only to continue adding to my technical knowledge, but also to make a difference with the skills that I have for the better.
